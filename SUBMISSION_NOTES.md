## Key Assumptions

- Coaches submit one score set per client each week.
- Predictive score below 5 requires concrete next-week actions.
- Leadership needs a fast risk snapshot, not a deep analytics suite.
- Prototype optimizes for speed and clarity over full production hardening.

## Intentionally Left Out

- Authentication and role-based access.
- Multi-user collaboration and real-time updates.
- Production DB migrations, audit logging, and governance controls.
- Full spreadsheet import pipeline and data cleaning UI.

## What I Would Build Next

- Per-coach account ownership with stricter row-level access policies.
- Trend visualizations and weekly deltas at client + portfolio levels.
- Automated alerts for score drops and prolonged risk periods.
- Rich AI summaries of weekly notes and recommended interventions.
